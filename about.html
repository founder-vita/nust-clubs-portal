About NUST Clubs Portal

The NUST Clubs Portal is a student-led initiative to centralize all societies, clubs, and student-run organizations under one platform.

Our goal is to make it easier for students to discover, connect, and participate in the vibrant extracurricular ecosystem at NUST.

Currently functioning only as a redireccting site;this portal is community-driven, and we're on our toes trying to improve it further!
